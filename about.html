My name is Luis Ramos, I am a student attending Cal State East Bay, anticipating to graduating in the Spring Semester of 2021 with an degree on Interaction, and Game Design. I design games through brainstorming ideas, organize the level environments, and prototyping the gameplay functions. I am familiar in utilizing Unreal Engine to develop a 2-D side scrolling game, and a 3-D Third Person adventure game. I independently recognize technical problems, and quickly resolve them. I thrive for different challenges to develop interactive games, and apply my skills within the game design industry.
